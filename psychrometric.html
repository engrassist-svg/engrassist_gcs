How to Use This Tool

🖱

Click Mode

Click anywhere on the chart to place a point. The tool automatically calculates all psychrometric properties using ASHRAE formulas.

⌨

Manual Entry

Select any two known variables and enter their values. The tool solves for all remaining properties and plots the point on the chart.

🔗

Process Lines

Enable process lines to connect points and visualize HVAC processes. View sensible heat ratio, capacity per CFM, and tonnage calculations.

🌡

Comfort Zone

Toggle the ASHRAE Standard 55 comfort zone overlay to visualize summer and winter thermal comfort boundaries on the chart.

Calculated Properties

  • Dry Bulb Temperature — Actual air temperature measured by standard thermometer
  • Wet Bulb Temperature — Temperature measured with wetted thermometer, indicates evaporative cooling potential
  • Dew Point Temperature — Temperature at which moisture begins to condense
  • Relative Humidity — Percentage of moisture in air relative to saturation
  • Humidity Ratio — Grains of water vapor per pound of dry air
  • Enthalpy — Total heat content of air in BTU per pound of dry air
  • Specific Volume — Cubic feet per pound of dry air
  • Vapor Pressure — Partial pressure of water vapor in the air

Process Analysis (with Process Lines enabled)

  • Process Type — Automatic classification (sensible heating/cooling, humidification, etc.)
  • Sensible Heat Ratio (SHR) — Ratio of sensible to total heat change
  • Capacity per CFM — Sensible, latent, and total BTU/hr per CFM of airflow
  • Tons per 1000 CFM — Cooling tonnage required per 1000 CFM
