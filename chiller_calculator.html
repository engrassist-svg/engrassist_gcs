Sizing Guidelines & Rules of Thumb

Commercial Building Cooling Load (BTU per Square Foot)

  • Office Buildings: 200-300 BTU/sq ft (or 40-60 sq ft per ton)
  • Retail Stores: 250-350 BTU/sq ft (or 35-50 sq ft per ton)
  • Restaurants: 350-500 BTU/sq ft (or 25-35 sq ft per ton)
  • Hotels/Residential: 200-300 BTU/sq ft (or 40-60 sq ft per ton)
  • Data Centers: 150-200 BTU/sq ft of IT space (highly variable)
  • Hospitals: 250-400 BTU/sq ft (or 30-50 sq ft per ton)
  • Warehouses (conditioned): 100-150 BTU/sq ft (or 80-120 sq ft per ton)

Safety Factors

  • Replacement Systems: 10-15%
  • New Construction: 15-20%
  • Future Expansion: 20-30%
  • Variable Speed Chillers: Can size closer to actual load due to part-load efficiency

Chiller Efficiency Reference

  • Air-Cooled Chillers: 9-12 EER (0.8-1.2 kW/ton) - easier installation, lower initial cost
  • Water-Cooled Screw: 11-15 EER (0.6-0.9 kW/ton) - good efficiency, moderate maintenance
  • Water-Cooled Centrifugal: 14-20+ EER (0.45-0.7 kW/ton) - highest efficiency, best for large loads
  • Magnetic Bearing Centrifugal: 18-25+ EER (0.35-0.5 kW/ton) - premium efficiency, oil-free operation
  • Variable Speed Drive (VSD): Improves part-load efficiency by 30-50%
