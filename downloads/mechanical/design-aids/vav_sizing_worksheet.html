Minimum Airflow Calculation

Minimum airflow is the GREATER of:

  1. Ventilation Requirement (ASHRAE 62.1):

    • Office: 5 CFM/person + 0.06 CFM/SF

  2. Minimum for Air Circulation:
    • Typically 30% of maximum CFM
    • Prevents stratification and maintains air movement
  3. Minimum for Duct Velocity:
    • Maintain minimum 500-700 FPM in duct
    • Prevents settling of dust and contaminants

Formula: Turndown Ratio = Maximum CFM √∑ Minimum CFM
