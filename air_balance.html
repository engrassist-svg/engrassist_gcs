  • Positive Pressurization: More outside air supplied than internal air exhausted. Prevents outdoor air infiltration. Typical: 3-10%
  • Neutral Pressurization: Balanced exhaust and outside air. Used when specific pressure control isn't critical.
  • Negative Pressurization: More air exhausted than outside air. Prevents contaminated air from spreading. Typical: 5-15%
