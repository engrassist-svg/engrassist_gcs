Sizing Guidelines & Rules of Thumb

Commercial Building BTU per Square Foot

  • Cold Climate (Northern US/Canada): 40-50 BTU/sq ft
  • Moderate Climate (Most of US): 30-40 BTU/sq ft
  • Warm Climate (Southern US): 20-30 BTU/sq ft
  • Well-Insulated Modern Buildings: 25-35 BTU/sq ft
  • Warehouses/High Ceilings: 50-75 BTU/sq ft
  • High-Ventilation Spaces: 75-100+ BTU/sq ft

Safety Factors

  • Replacement Boilers: 10-15%
  • New Construction: 15-20%
  • Future Expansion: 20-25%
  • Modulating Boilers: Can size closer to actual load due to turndown ratio
