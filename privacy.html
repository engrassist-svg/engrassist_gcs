Privacy Summary: EngrAssist.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal personal information, use it only for providing our services, and never sell your data to third parties. Our tools can be used without creating an account, and we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect the following information when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, company/school name when you contact us or request information
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and support requests you send us
  • Account Information: Username, email, and preferences if you create a premium account (future feature)
  • Professional Information: Role, industry, and engineering background when voluntarily provided

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, and tool usage patterns
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system
  • Performance Data: Page load times, errors, and site performance metrics
  • Referral Data: How you found our website (search engines, direct links, etc.)

1.3 Tool Usage Data

Our HVAC calculation tools collect:

  • Input Values: Numbers and parameters you enter into calculators (stored locally in your browser only)
  • Calculation Results: Outputs generated by our tools (not stored on our servers)
  • Tool Performance: Which tools are used most frequently for improvement purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain our HVAC calculation tools
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Process contact form submissions
  • Deliver educational content and resources

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze tool usage to identify popular features
  • Fix bugs and improve tool accuracy
  • Develop new tools based on user needs
  • Optimize website performance and user experience

2.3 Communication

  • Send responses to your inquiries
  • Provide technical support and assistance
  • Send important updates about our services (with consent)
  • Share educational content related to HVAC engineering (opt-in only)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3.1 Limited Sharing Circumstances

We may share information only in these specific situations:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (email services, analytics, hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: To protect our users, prevent fraud, or investigate security incidents
  • Business Transfer: If EngrAssist is acquired or merged (users will be notified)

3.2 Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website usage analysis (anonymized data)
  • Email Services: For processing contact form submissions
  • Cloud Hosting: Google Cloud Platform for website hosting

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Hosting: Data stored on secure, professionally managed servers
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates applied regularly

4.2 Data Minimization

  • We collect only information necessary for our services
  • Tool calculations are processed locally in your browser when possible
  • Personal information is retained only as long as necessary
  • Unused accounts and data are automatically deleted

5. Cookies and Tracking

5.1 Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how our site is used (anonymized)

5.2 Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at engrassist@gmail.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is designed for professional engineers, engineering students, and adults in related fields. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
